Meet Jasmine

Jasmine Lee started her foray into journalism by writing for a local publication called Naked Underground Toronto in her last year of high school. For the next two to three years, she grew her experience through interviewing different musical acts, reviewing albums, and photographing concerts and music festivals. In the latter years of her time spent in university, she wrote many think pieces on politics and international relations. While she does not exclusively focus on one region or issue, she is passionate about what she has written. She has worked and/or studied in five different countries across the globe in the last two years, and has recently finished her Bachelor of Arts degree at McGill University. She hopes to continue expanding her horizons through her travel and explorational projects as she delves into her professional career.
